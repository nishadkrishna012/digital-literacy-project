Social Media Do’s and Don’ts (Final)

✔️ Do’s

Maintain a professional and clean profile
Think before posting any content
Use strong privacy settings
Verify information before sharing
Be respectful in online communication

❌ Don’ts

Don’t share personal or sensitive information
Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content
Don’t engage in cyberbullying or arguments
Avoid accepting unknown friend requests
Don’t overshare your location or daily routine
