Prevention Checklist

🔒 General Safety Tips

Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts
Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible
Do not click on suspicious links or unknown emails (phishing)
Keep your devices and apps updated regularly
Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive transactions

💳 Financial / UPI Safety Tips

Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or banking details with anyone
Always verify payment requests before approving them
Use only trusted apps like official banking or verified UPI apps
Check the receiver’s name carefully before sending money

🧑‍💻 Personal Data Protection

Do not share personal information (Aadhaar, phone number, address) publicly
Be cautious while downloading files or apps from unknown sources
Review app permissions before installing

🚨 Awareness & Reporting

Be aware of common scams like phishing, fake job offers, and lottery frauds
Immediately report any cybercrime on the National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in)
Call Helpline Number: 1930 in case of financial fraud
